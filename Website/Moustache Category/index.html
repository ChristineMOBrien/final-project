Moustache Category

The moustache is facial hair grown on the upper lip.

The word "moustache" is French, and is derived from the Italian moustacio, dialectal mostaccio, from Medieval Latin moustaccium, Medieval Greek moustakion, attested in the ninth century, which ultimately originates as a diminutive of Hellenistic Greek, meaning "upper lip" or "facial hair".

Often times in the competition, Moustaches are be groomed by trimming and styling with a type of pomade called moustache wax.

As of the World Beard and Moustache Championships 2007, there are six sub-categories for moustaches - Natural, English, Dali, Imperial, Hungarian, and the Freestyle.
