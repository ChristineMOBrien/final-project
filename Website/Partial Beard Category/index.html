Partial Beard Category

The Partial Beards category includes any number of styles for carved facial hair. Note that they differ in particular aspects from the full beard category and therefore seperate. The categories for WBMC competitions are Natural, Fu Manchu, Imperial, Musketeer, Sideburns, and Amish Beard.
