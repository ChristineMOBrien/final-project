The World Beard and Moustache Championships

Welcome! My website is designed to guide you through the groundwork of The World Beard and Moustache Championships.

This unique sporting event brings together the best beardsmen in the world to compete for grand prizes and show off their artistic facial hair. The World Beard and Moustache Championships is a biennial competition hosted by the World Beard and Moustache Association (WBMA) in which men with beards and moustaches display lengthy, highly styled facial hair. There are 3 brackets of facial hair: Moustache, Partial Beard and Full Beard. Each bracket is broken into individual categories. There are usually 17 categories but there were 18 different categories for the 2009 championships.

Check out how it started, how the competition is layed out, and the sponsorships of the event!

In 2015 the WBMC returns to Europe, when the Alpine village of Leogang, Austria plays host. The East Bavarian Beard Club will organize the event. Beard Team USA plans to attend after stopping over in Munich at Oktoberfest.
